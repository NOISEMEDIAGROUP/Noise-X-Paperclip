## Configuration Notes

### Automation Tool Setup
Use these emails in Brevo, Mailchimp, Klaviyo, or equivalent:
1. Create "Customer Onboarding" automation
2. Trigger: Purchase completed
3. Set up email sequence with timing
4. Conditional sends for surveys (based on feedback)

### Personalization Variables
- `[First Name]` → Customer's first name
- `[DATE]` → 30 days from purchase date
- `[Launch brand studio]` → Link to questionnaire/dashboard

### A/B Testing Ideas
- Subject line variations for open rates
- Call-to-action button vs. link
- Email length (some prefer shorter, some longer)
- Send times (morning vs. evening)

### Tracking
Measure:
- Open rates (target: 40%+)
- Click rates (target: 15%+)
- Survey response rate
- Refund rate (target: < 5%)
- Time to first logo (target: < 5 min)

## Philosophy

**These emails have one job: Remove friction.**

Every email should:
✅ Answer a likely question
✅ Encourage action (but don't nag)
✅ Provide a next step
✅ Feel personal, not robotic

If an email makes someone feel like they made a mistake buying, delete it. We're here to help them succeed.
