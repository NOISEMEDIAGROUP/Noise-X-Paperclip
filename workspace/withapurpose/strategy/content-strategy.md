## BRAND VOICE

### Core Attributes

| Attribute | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Direct** | No fluff, get to the point |
| **Competent** | Know the subject matter |
| **Strategic** | Think ahead, connect dots |
| **Human** | Conversational, not robotic |
| **Fresh** | Only current, relevant content |

### Voice Guidelines

**DO:**
- Lead with insight, not setup
- Use concrete examples and numbers
- Write like briefing a smart colleague
- Acknowledge uncertainty when real
- Be genuinely useful

**DON'T:**
- Over-explain or hedge unnecessarily
- Use robotic phrasing or corporate speak
- Post about anything older than 7 days
- Chase every trend - pick what matters
- Use em dashes excessively

## POSTING RULES

### 7-DAY FRESHNESS RULE (HARD CUTOFF)

- Only post about news/events from the LAST 7 DAYS
- Anything older is dead content
- No exceptions, no "this is still relevant"
- When in doubt, check the date

### Content Quality Standards

1. **Lead with value** - First line must hook
2. **One idea per post** - Don't cram
3. **Actionable when possible** - Give readers something to do
4. **Own your takes** - Don't just reshare, add perspective
5. **No paid ads** - Organic growth only

### What NOT to Post

- Personal life details (teaching, twins, location)
- Anything older than 7 days
- Generic AI hype without substance
- Promotional content without value
- Repetitive same-link spam
